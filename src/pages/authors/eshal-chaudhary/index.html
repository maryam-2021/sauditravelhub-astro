About Eshal

Eshal is the Travel and Culture Editor responsible for Saudi Travel Hub's destination guides, visa walkthroughs, and cultural content. She researches, writes, and fact-checks the site's most in-depth editorial pieces — from eVisa application guides to city breakdowns and food coverage.

She monitors Saudi Tourism Authority updates and MOFA policy changes daily to keep all regulatory content current. Her expertise lies in bridging the gap between official regulations and the practical needs of independent travellers.
