About Saudi Travel Hub

Independent editorial travel guide to the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia — who we are, how we work, and why you can trust our content

Home / About Us

"Saudi Travel Hub exists to give independent travellers accurate, unsponsored, first-hand guidance on visiting Saudi Arabia — a destination that has transformed faster than any other in the world over the past five years."

Saudi Travel Hub is an independent editorial guide founded in 2023, covering Saudi Arabia's destinations, visa process, culture, food, and upcoming FIFA 2034 travel planning. We are not affiliated with the Saudi government, Saudi Tourism Authority, or any tour operator. Our content is editorially independent.

We have personally visited every major destination covered on this site — from the Nabataean tombs of Hegra in AlUla to the coral reefs of the Red Sea, the UNESCO streets of Al-Balad in Jeddah, and the lava fields of Harrat Khaybar. Our visa guidance is based on direct application experience and verified against official Saudi Tourism Authority sources.

Our editorial standards

  • All factual claims are sourced and cited — statistics reference official sources (Saudi Tourism Authority, UNESCO, SCTH) rather than unverified aggregators.
  • Visa and entry information is verified against official Saudi Tourism Authority and MOFA sources, with update dates clearly marked.
  • Destination guides are written from personal on-the-ground experience — we do not publish AI-generated content without editorial verification.
  • We do not accept payment to promote hotels, tours, or services. Booking links are for reader convenience and may generate affiliate revenue — this never influences editorial coverage.
  • Content is reviewed and updated quarterly, or immediately following official policy changes (such as visa regulation updates).

Commercial disclosure

Affiliate links: Some booking links on Saudi Travel Hub (hotels, tours, flights) are affiliate links — if you book through them, we receive a small commission at no extra cost to you. This does not influence which properties, services, or destinations we recommend. Our editorial content is never sponsored or paid for.

Advertising: Saudi Travel Hub displays contextual advertising. Advertisers have no influence over editorial content.

Not affiliated: Saudi Travel Hub is an independent guide. We are not affiliated with the Saudi Tourism Authority, the Saudi government, Visit Saudi, or any tour operator.
