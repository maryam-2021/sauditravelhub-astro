Editorial Standards

How we research, write, and verify travel information about the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia

Home › Editorial Standards

Our commitment: Every piece of content on Saudi Travel Hub is written by humans with direct experience of Saudi Arabia, verified against official sources, and updated regularly. We are editorially independent.

Independence

Saudi Travel Hub is not affiliated with the Saudi Tourism Authority, the Saudi government, Visit Saudi, or any tour operator or travel agency. Our editorial decisions are made independently. We do not accept payment to promote hotels, tours, or destinations.

When we link to booking services, we may earn an affiliate commission at no extra cost to you. This income keeps the guide free and updated. Affiliate relationships never influence which properties or services we recommend.

Research & accuracy

All factual claims on Saudi Travel Hub are verified against primary sources:

  • Visa & entry requirements: Verified against the Saudi eVisa portal and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MOFA)
  • Tourism data: Cross-referenced with the Saudi Tourism Authority and UNESCO
  • FIFA 2034: Sourced from FIFA.com official announcements
  • Transport & infrastructure: Verified against Saudi Arabia Railways and official operator sites

We clearly mark any information that is estimated, projected, or subject to change (such as future pricing, opening dates, or policy changes). Every guide page displays a "Last Updated" date.

Experience-led content

Our writers are required to have personally visited the destinations they cover. We do not publish destination guides based solely on desk research. Every guide includes first-hand observations, practical tips, and photographs that can only come from being on the ground.

Our editorial team has collectively spent over 3 years living in and travelling across Saudi Arabia, from the UNESCO streets of Al-Balad in Jeddah to the Nabataean tombs of Hegra in AlUla, the lava fields of Harrat Khaybar, and the coral reefs of the Red Sea.

Content freshness

Saudi Arabia is changing faster than almost any country on earth. Information that was accurate six months ago may no longer be correct. To address this:

  • All guide pages are reviewed and updated quarterly, or immediately following official policy changes
  • Every page displays a visible "Last Updated" date
  • Visa and regulatory pages are monitored for policy changes and updated within 48 hours of official announcements

Named authorship

Every article and guide on Saudi Travel Hub is attributed to a named author. We believe that putting a human face and real credentials behind content is essential for trust. You can learn more about our team on the About page.

Corrections policy

How to report an error

If you spot a factual error on any page, please contact us at editorial@sauditravelhub.com. We take accuracy seriously.

How we handle corrections

  • We acknowledge all correction requests within 24 hours
  • Verified factual errors are corrected within 48 hours
  • Corrections are noted at the bottom of the relevant article with a date stamp
  • We do not silently edit content — material changes are always disclosed

AI-assisted content disclosure

Some research, data analysis, and drafting on Saudi Travel Hub may use AI-assisted tools. However:

  • All published content is reviewed, edited, and verified by human editors with direct experience of Saudi Arabia
  • AI tools are never used as sole sources of factual claims
  • Final editorial judgment always rests with our named human authors
